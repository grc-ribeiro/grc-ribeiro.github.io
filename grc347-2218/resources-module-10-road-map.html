GRC 347: Resources

Module 10

Line extension

Learning objectives

In this module we will work on how to:

  1. Create more realistic representations of packaging.

Before the lab

  1. Watch the video critique on the second round of line extensions.

  2. Read the class journal highlights from last week.

Entry ticket

Before you come to class you need to complete the following:

  1. Answer the one-question survey on the video critique on the second round of line extensions.

  2. Answer the one-question survey on the class journal highlights from last week.

Due the night before lab at 11:59pm.

Come prepared and energized to work on your final prototypes!

In the lab

  1. In the first 20 to 30 minutes of the lab, you’ll be meeting with your peer support team to share progress from last week, discuss this week’s assignment, and establish strategies for success in this week’s lab.

  2. The remaining of the class will be dedicated to working on your line extension.

  3. In the last 30 minutes of class, you’ll meet with your group again to share your progress in today’s lab and get feedback to improve your work before submitting it to Canvas.

After the lab

Submit the assignment. (Deadline assigned on Canvas.)

And don’t forget to submit your journals!
