Road map: Module 1

Welcome to GRC 347

Branding

Learning objectives

In this module we will work on how to:

  1. Research the competition for the established conventions as well as for innovation opportunities.

Before the lab

  1. Read the welcome letter on Canvas.

  2. Watch the video on branding and complete its quiz on Canvas.

Entry ticket

Before you come to class you need to complete the following:

  1. Take the initial survey.

  2. Finish the quiz on the topic of branding.

In the lab

  1. In the first 20 to 30 minutes of the lab, we’ll have some quick introductions and establish some ground rules for the quarter.

  2. The remaining of the class will be dedicated to get familiarized with your project and its domain.

  3. In the last 40 to 60 minutes of class, we’ll establish a system of peer support to foster collaboration in the lab. (I know you’ve missed interacting with other students. I have too.)

After the lab

Submit the assignment. (Deadline assigned on Canvas.)

And don’t forget to submit your journals!
