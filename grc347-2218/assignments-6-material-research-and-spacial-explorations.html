GRC 347: Assignments

Material research and spacial explorations

Based on your research of materials, explain what material and processes you plan on using in one product of your line of products. Explain the your decisions and make sure to include considerations on costs and environmental impact.

Include rough illustrations (sketches or digital) for possibilities on what the packaging will look like.

Requirements

  1. Start be restating the name, the tagline, and a brief explanation of the product line and the brand voice.

  2. Choose one product from your line of products.

  3. Show your research on materials, showing examples from commercial products or concepts from other companies that show possible solution that you could use in this specific product.

  4. Show at least three possibilities of shapes through hand illustration, digital illustration, or photographs of rough paper mockups.

  5. Include at least three pros and at least three cons for every possible solution.

Format

  1. Letter size portrait (vertical).

  2. Use a nice sans-serif typeface. Arial is never a nice typeface.

  3. No colors on the type. Keep it simple.

  4. Paragraph lines that are around 11 to 14 words long.

  5. Align to left, with no indentation.

  6. No hyphenation either.

  7. Use proper hierarchy. (e.g.: Bold for headings.)

I suggest you use the same from your creative brief.
