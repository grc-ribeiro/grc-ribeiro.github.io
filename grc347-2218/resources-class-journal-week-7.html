Does center aligning elements on a package make it more difficult to read than left aligning it?

No. They just look worse. Unless, it’s a paragraph, then it’s more difficult to read and they look worse.

What are some great sans-serif typefaces to use for product information text?

I have just suggested these on GRC 322: Acumin, Aktiv Grotesk, Aller, Avenir, Bell Gothic, DIN, Effra, Foco, Franklin Gothic, Frutiger, Futura, Gotham, Inter, Interface, Interstate, Meta, Mr Eaves XL, Officina Sans, P22 Underground, Rotis Sans, Trade Gothic, Univers, and Vectora.
