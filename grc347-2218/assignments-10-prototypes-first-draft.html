Peer-review

Once again, we’ll be using the Canvas’s peer‑review. Each of will be assigned to five reviews. They’re not required or graded. You’ll be doing it voluntarily to help your peers.

If we were in the labs, you’ll be in groups giving each other feedback, but since it’s not possible, I’d like to see you all helping each other. I know that many of you miss it too.

Also, don’t use the rubric. Just leave comments stating:

  1. What’s your favorite and why.

  2. Suggestions for improvements.

Have fun!

Peer‑reviews are loosely due on Wednesday, 17 March 2021, at 11:59pm. But since they’re not required, just get them done whenever you can; I’m sure your peers will appreciate getting them late rather than never.
