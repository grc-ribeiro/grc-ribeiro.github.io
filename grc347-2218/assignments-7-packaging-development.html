GRC 347: Assignments

Packaging development

Based on your visual ideations and spacial explorations, develop at least three digital options for one product of your line of products.

At this point, the “mockups” will be very crude. You don’t need to make them realistic. You want to communicate the idea for how the packaging/label will be.

Evaluation

There is no graded deliverable this week.

Just have fun developing three different ideas for your packaging!

Submission

A three‑page, letter-size, portrait PDF, in order of preference from your favorite to your third favorite.

Submit your PDF to Canvas Thursday, 4 November 2021, at 11:59pm.
