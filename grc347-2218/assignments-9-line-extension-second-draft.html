GRC 347: Assignments

Line extension (second draft)

Based on comments you got from me and your peers, refine the packaging design for all the products in your product line.

At this point, you may want to work with more realistic prototype tools, but it’s not a requirement.

Tasks

  1. Read the comments on Canvas.

  2. Refine your work.

  3. Submit multi-page PDFs showing the products individually and as a group.

Some of you have started worked in 3D models, which is awesome, but it’s not a requirement.

Evaluation

  • At least three products

    20 points

    Simple count

    • 20 pts Yes
    • 0 pts No

    40 points

  • Consistency

    20 points

    Products are consistent enough to be recognized as part of the same line of products.

    • 20 pts Satisfactory
    • 15 pts Mostly satisfactory
    • 10 pts Partially satisfactory
    • 0 pts Unsatisfactory

  • Similarity and differentiation

    20 points

    Products are different enough to be recognized as different products, even though they belong to the same line.

    • 20 pts Satisfactory
    • 15 pts Mostly satisfactory
    • 10 pts Partially satisfactory
    • 0 pts Unsatisfactory 20 pts
