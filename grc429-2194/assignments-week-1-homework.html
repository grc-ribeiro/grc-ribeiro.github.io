GRC 429: Assignments

Homework (week 1)

I believe that coming up with an idea for the project is the biggest challenge in this course. That’s why we have this homework activity. It will be graded as if it was the report update for week 1.

In this course, you will work for 10 weeks on the same project. It can be a web service, a smartphone app, a kiosk interface, or anything that has a visual interaction interface for the final user to interact with. (You don’t need, however, to limit yourself to devices you already own or know.)

Think about emerging technology, even if it’s not feasible commercially. (Tele-transportation is off-limits, but wireless electricity is fair game.) Here are some ideas for emerging technology you can work with. You may, of course, combine more than one item from the list.

  1. Ultra fast wireless internet
    Think 100TB/s or more.

  2. Wireless electricity
    Not only at home or at work, but on the streets, everywhere.

  3. Self-driven cars
    What are the possibilities beyond checking Facebook while commuting?

  4. Wearable devices of all kinds
    Not only glasses or watches. Think about necklesses, rings, shoes, whatever…

  5. Smart home
    What if everything at your home was connected to the internet?

  6. Augmented reality
    How technology can give us the information that we need on each context?

Come up with 12 to 20 ideas. Think about different devices, scales, and kinds of product. Think about your own interests as well as other people’s (travel, sports, studies, music, camping, etc.)

 

Submit your ideas on PolyLearn (as plain text) and bring two hard copies to class to your lab in week 2. (April 8th or 10th.)
