GRC 429: Assignments

Final presentation

Design a presentation to teach us about your product.

You want to tell a story of what people can do with your app. What does it do? How does it do it? Why is it different? Also, include your process, personas, ethical consideration, future enhancements, etc.

You want to present it as if you were in a job interview with a potential employer. (Rather than a presentation to a fictional consumer or user of your product.)

You may have as many slides as you need, but you will have 10 minutes to present either on June 3rd or June 5th, during lab.

Save your presentation as a 16×9 ratio (e.g. 1920×1080 pixels), landscape, multi-page PDF. (You may present it from whatever software you choose, though.)

Presentation slides

  1. Title page

    Name of the product, name of the students and date of presentation.
    You may include an image of the product or maybe only a related image.

  2. Brief description

    Your one-line description with an image.

  3. State the problem

    Briefly. Images always help.

  4. Persona

    Name, photo, quote, background, and goals.

  5. Show your solution

    What does it do? How does it do it? Why’s it different?

Submit your presentation as PDF to PolyLearn, by Thursday, 6 June 2019 at 11:55pm, using the name convention lastname1-lastname2-presentation.pdf. Use alphabetical order by last name. And mind the spelling of all names.
