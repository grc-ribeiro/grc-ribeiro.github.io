How to take good screenshots of webpages on a Mac

  1. Open the webpage on its own window, with no other tabs.

  2. Hide the Favorites Bar.

  3. Hide the Tab Bar.

  4. Press cmd+shift+4 on your Mac.

  5. Press the space bar.

  6. Take the picture of your browser window using your mouse.
