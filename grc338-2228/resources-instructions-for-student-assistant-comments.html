Week 2

Restaurant menu

Student submissions

List of mistakes to avoid

Download Firefox to look at the page source. It helps you by highlighting the errors in red.

Reminder: use gender neutral language when leaving comments! (i.e. they/them/their)

Estimated time: 2–3 hours

Grading criteria: HTML

body (all the visible content needs to be in the body of the document)

finaltags (didn’t close the final tags, body and/or html)

head (no head in the document)

hrr (Hierarchy is off.)*

listitems (only list items can be inside lists)

lists (list items need to be nested inside lists)

org (Code is not organized, no need to include line numbers)

title (Didn’t include an appropriate title)

gw (Good work, no comments, no need to include line numbers)

  • You may need to include further explanation. Make two slashes (//) and write the comment to me.

Examples:

    * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * *

    Alex Doe

    org

    4 body

    7–9 hrr // made h2 where it should be h1 and vice-versa

    86 finaltags

    * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * *

    Sam Smith

    gw

    * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * *

Meaning that…

Alex’s code was not very organized;

in the line 4 of their document, they had visible elements in the head rather than the body;

In lines 7 to 9, the hierarchy was off, because they used h1 where it should be h2 and vice-versa;

they forgot to include the closing body and html tags in the end of the document.

While Sam’s work was flawless.

List of mistakes to avoid

Estimated time: 3–4 hours

  • Bolded grading criteria are new additions

Grading criteria: HTML

(They were still not supposed to change the HTML, just the CSS and replace the image file, but now, it’s for the desktop/laptop layout.)

Grading criteria: CSS

img (images not showing or it’s the placeholder)

gw (Good work, no comments, no need to include line numbers)

org (Code is not organized, no need to include line numbers)

rgb (Declare all colors as color values. Don’t use the color names.)

psize (Paragraphs (p) must be at least 1em.)

px (Don’t use px or pt for anything but borders.)

neg (Don’t use negative margins or negative paddings).

Then, a few details:

Students are supposed to include a max-width (in em) and margin: auto for the div.container.

They’re not supposed to include any grid property for the div.

Both main and figure are supposed to have the grid properties.

Both main and figure need display: grid on each.

They will need two values for the grid-template-column on each element too.
