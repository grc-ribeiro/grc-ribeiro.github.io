I was reminded to pay attention to the design principles we learned when designing our website. It’s one thing to know what the principles are; it’s another to actually apply them in your designs. Speaking of design principles, I feel like certain principles come naturally to anyone with an eye for design. You can look at something and say “this looks wrong/right” and it’s because you know (but might not be actively conscious of) the principles.

That’s right. Some principles do come naturally. But using them consciously help you make decisions. To design is to make decisions.
