GRC 377: Assignments

Movie poster

Use Adobe InDesign to create a poster for the same movie you created a logo and a minimalist poster in the first week of class. Please follow the guidelines below:

  1. 24 × 36 inches (w × h)

  2. Use one image as background, in a separate layer. The image needs to be, at least, 2,400-pixel wide and 3,600-pixel tall.

  3. Include the logo you designed in the first week of class. (You can change the color, or make small adjusts.)

  4. Include the name of one actor, at least.

  5. Include the name of the director.

  6. You’re not required to include more information than that, but it will certainly increase your grade for challenge.
