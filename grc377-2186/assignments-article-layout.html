ART 377: Assignments

Article layout

Create a new document in InDesign. Don’t use facing pages.

Size: Letter (8.5″ × 11″)

Margins:
Top: 1.5″
Bottom: 1.0″
Left: 1.5″
Right: 1.0″

Download this article and paste its content to InDesign.

Text frames should touch all margins and have only one column.

Create four Paragraph Styles:

  1. Title

    Helvetica Bold, 24 pt (size) / 28.8 pt (leading)
    Normal Case
    Aligned to Left (not justified)
    Space after paragraph: 14.4 pt
    Don’t hyphenate
    Color: Black

  2. Author

    Helvetica Oblique, 12 pt (size) / 14.4 pt (leading)
    Normal Case
    Aligned to Left (not justified)
    Space after paragraph: 14.4 pt
    Don’t hyphenate
    Color: Black

  3. Body

    Adobe Garamond, 12 pt (size) / 14.4 pt (leading)
    Normal Case
    Aligned to Left (not justified)
    Space after paragraph: 14.4 pt
    Don’t hyphenate
    Color: Black
    Keep all lines in paragraph together

  4. Caption

    Helvetica Regular, 10 pt (size) / 14.4 pt (leading)
    Normal Case
    Aligned to Left (not justified)
    Space after paragraph: 14.4 pt
    Don’t hyphenate
    Color: Black

Apply Title to title, Author to author, and Body to the rest of the text.

 

Create two Character Styles:

  1. Italic

    Helvetica Oblique

  2. Credits

    Helvetica Regular, 8 pt (size)
    Case: All Caps

 

Apply Italic to the phrases Psychological Science in the Public Interest and Applied Cognitive Psychology.

 

Include images

Go to Unsplash and find from three to five images to go with the article. Download them and place them on your file. Don’t forget to include captions, with credits to the photographer.

You want your captions and your images to be on the same frame.

Then, you want to link the frame on the text.

Apply the paragraph style Caption to the captions.

Apply the paragraph style Credits to the photographer credits on the captions.

 

Create a new Master Page (B)

Include Current Page Number.

Aligned to the right margin. Distant one inch from top and right edges of the page.

Apply Master B to all pages except the first.

Make sure you included all the text!!!
