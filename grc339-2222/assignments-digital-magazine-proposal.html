GRC 339: Assignments

Digital magazine proposal

For this assignment, you need to create a website for a news publication. This publication has a specific focus rather than broad general news or even topics such as fashion, sports, or music. Think specific genre of fashion or music, or maybe a specific league or even a team.

Examples of focus for the publication:

  1. Vegetable home growing
  2. Young women in business
  3. American street art
  4. Underground hip hop
  5. NBA
  6. Manchester United
  7. Bird watchers
  8. Calligraphy and lettering
  9. etc., etc., etc.

Please, don’t limit yourself to the options above. They were just examples.

You can work in groups of one or two students.

Brand voice and reference board

  1. Start with brands in other industries that “speak” the visual language that you want to apply to your brand. (Think: “If my brand was a car, it would be…”, “If it was a restaurant…”)

  2. Expand the search with non-branded images with the appropriate look-and-feel of your brand. (Design, art, anything really.) You want to have a mix of material, ranging from photographs to (typo)graphic material.

  3. Use Pinterest or a similar service to collect the images.

    Important: Although Pinterest is great for collecting images, it is awful tool for searching for images. Do not use Pinterest to look for your images. Find better sources and upload them to Pinterest yourself.

  4. Select the images to make a cohesive collection. Include somewhere from 30 to 80 images on your board. (There’s no penalty for doing more as long as it’s still a cohesive collection.)

Tasks

  1. Write an introduction to your magazine, with its name and its focus.

  2. Choose three to eight words that you would associate with your brand voice. But don’t use only words directly related to the product such as “vegetable” or “basketball”, you want to include some words that are more subjectively related to your brand.

  3. Generate a preliminary color palette on Coolors or any similar service with shareable links for specific themes. (I don’t think Adobe Color has this feature, but I’d like to learn that I’m wrong.) You want your reference board to inform you color palette.

  4. Choose two or three type families from Adobe Fonts for your publication. Again, you want your reference board to inform your type selection.

  5. Download this template and populate it with the content of your proposal.

Groups

This assignment was designed to be an individual project. But since some of you are already collaborating with another student, I want to encourage you to keep collaborating. Therefore, you may choose between groups of one or two students. But there are two simple rules:

  1. If you choose to go solo, don’t complain about the amount of work. The assignment was designed to be accomplished by one person.

  2. If you choose to work with someone else, don’t complain about your teammate not working as much as you are. The assignment was designed to be accomplished by one person, and the choice of working with someone else is yours.

Submission

  1. Go to your grc339/digital-magazine-proposal/ folder in your public repository on GitHub. ➤

  2. Upload your HTML file to your GitHub repository. ➤

  3. Click on your name to check if it’s working.

  4. If it’s working, submit to Canvas the emoji that best represents your magazine.

(If you’re working in a group of two, one student of the group submits the proposal, while the other submits a redirect file to their teammate’s proposal. Both submit an emoji to Canvas.)
