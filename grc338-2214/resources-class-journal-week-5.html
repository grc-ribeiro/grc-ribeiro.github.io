I also learned the conversion ratio of point to em, so I was able to change all of my font sizes to em measurements.

Forget about conversion ratio of pt to em. First, it’s the wrong mindset. Just use 1em for paragraph (it’s the default), and then think of the other sizes in relation to the paragraph. Second, the conversion ratio from pt to em is not always the same. If it was, we would use pt or px.
