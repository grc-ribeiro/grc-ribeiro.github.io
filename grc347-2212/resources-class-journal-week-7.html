Class journal, week 7

Deciding on packaging is so hard. I’m looking forward to the peer critiques to help solidify my choices.

Try to make to my office hours if you need help.

Meeting with you helped solidify my top 3 ideas.

I always enjoy my meetings with students. Not only was the meeting productive, but it was good to chat a bit.

Adobe Dimension is a bit tricky to use, Google has been my best friend when trying to work on some mock ups!

LinkedIn Learn can also help!

It has not been easy creating a product by myself as I feel like I can become shortsighted. Having a team with who you can bounce ideas off is very beneficial and helpful.

It’s never easy to design alone. Let’s try to forge at least some collaboration.

Peer feedback can be super helpful, giving you a better understanding of how your design is perceived by others, as well as providing you with ideas on how to improve it.

Check out Freepik for some free, high-quality mockups and other resources!

Thanks for sharing, Rafael!

Is it difficult for UX/UI concentrations/designers to dive into a packaging design job? What would the requirements be?

Not difficult at all. Especially with the knowledge you have from GRC 337 and this class.

I find it a bit hard to “think outside of the box” when it comes to packaging.

Don’t try to be original; just try to be good.
