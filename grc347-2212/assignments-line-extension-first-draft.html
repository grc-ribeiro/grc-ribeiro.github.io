GRC 347: Assignments

Line extension (first draft)

Extend your packaging to the other products in your product line. You may have a total of three to five products in your line.

You want to consider how to keep consistency throughout the line of products still making them distinguishable internally and agains the competition.

Some of you have made the decision on what shape and visual language to use. Some of you have not. Either way, I encourage you to set up an appointment with me during my office hours so we can discuss your ideas. Even more effective and fun is to set the appointment with more than one student, but neither is a requirement.

Tasks

  1. Watch the videos on line extensions.

  2. Choose one direction for your visual language.

  3. Open your mood board to check your references. Add more images if you find it necessary.

  4. Design three to five different products in your ilne.

  5. Submit multi-page PDFs showing the products individually and as a group.

Some of you have started worked in 3D models, which is awesome, but it’s not a requirement.

Evaluation

Check rubric for evaluation criteria.

(If you don’t see a rubric, please let me know as soon as possible.)

Submission

Submit a multi-page, letter-size, landscape PDF.

Submit your PDF to Canvas Thursday, 25 February 2021, at 11:59pm.

Peer-review

For this assignment, I’ll be using the Canvas’s peer‑review tool for the first time (ever). Each of will be assigned to five reviews. They’re not required or graded. You’ll be doing it voluntarily to help your peers.

If we were in the labs, you’ll be in groups giving each other feedback, but since it’s not possible, I’d like to see you all helping each other. I know that many of you miss it too.

Also, don’t use the rubric. Just leave comments stating:

  1. What’s your favorite and why.

  2. Suggestions for improvements.

Have fun!

Peer‑reviews are loosely due on Monday, 1 March 2021, at 11:59pm. But since they’re not required, just get them done whenever you can; I’m sure your peers will appreciate getting them late rather than never.
