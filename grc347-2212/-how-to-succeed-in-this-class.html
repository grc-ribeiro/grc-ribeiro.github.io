Welcome to GRC 347 (Packaging Graphics Technology and Design). The class begins on January 4th and ends on March 19th, when the final project and final exam will be due.

In this class, you will work on a ten‑week project, following a process with weekly milestones and deliverables to help you finish with a piece you will be very proud of showing in your portfolio.

GRC 347 is a fully online, asynchronous class, where you will be able to adapt it to your own schedule, but you will need to be on top of it. You are expected to work 9 hours a week in a 3-unit class, and we have consistent deliverables every week. Please check Canvas constantly and stay ahead of schedule.

 

Tips to be successful in this class

  1. Have a schedule. My online classes always have a consistent week schedule so you can plan ahead.

  2. Read the assignment instructions very carefully. I suggest you to read them sooner rather than later so you have time to ask the questions you may have.

  3. If you don’t understand the assignment requirements, ask; don’t assume.

  4. Show up to my virtual office hours.

 

Office hours

If you need my help, you can use my office hours as much as you need and you can also send me an email or a message through Canvas if you prefer—but sometimes the readiness of office hours is more efficient. I will make every effort to respond to your email within 24 hours during the week, but I don’t usually respond to emails on weekends.
