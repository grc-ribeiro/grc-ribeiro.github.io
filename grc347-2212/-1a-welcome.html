Course objectives

^ In ten weeks or so, you will be able…

  • To discuss how language, colors, customs, aesthetics, and placement affect global branding and packaging for products.

  • To write a creative brief and expalin its purpose and need.

  • To develop brand story for package using appropriate design criteria.

  • To present your design work.
