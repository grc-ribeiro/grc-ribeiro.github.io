GRC 347: Resources

Class journal, week 2

This week I learned how brands use design and colors to express their voice and image. It is important to keep your brand consistent and to make changes only if it will increase sales.

I am positively sure I didn’t mention anything about increasing sales. Keep your brand consistent and make changes when it makes sense.

A lack of conscious references leads to unconscious copying.

One of the main points of the class.

Should we always utilize a mood board before creating a new design? Are there instances when we don’t use it?

I always use them. Because a lack of conscious references leads to unconscious copying.

Learned how hard mood boards can be. I always thought they were easy to make but a lot of thought is put into each item on the board.

They are only actually helpful if you put thought on the task.

Using Pinterest I was able to create a mood board for the oral care company, but I haven’t decided on a color scheme yet. I think it might come easier once we start the design process. I am happy with how the mood board came out.

It probably will. You don’t make final decisions on the mood board.

I fell in love with Jessica Hische’s instagram!

And she’s awesome! I’ve met her twice, when she presented at Cal Poly in 2018 and at the AIGA conference in 2019.

I’m finding myself grow more excitement towards packaging with this project; something I haven’t really felt since being at Cal Poly.

I look forward to seeing your best work.
