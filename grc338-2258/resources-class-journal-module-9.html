What do the units in div.container { min-height: 100vh } mean?

From Sitepoint: “vh stands for viewport height. This unit is based on the height of the viewport. A value of 1vh is equal to 1% of the viewport height. A value of 100vh is equal to 100% of the viewport height.” So what it’s doing is ensuring that the div.container will be at least as tall as the browser window. But it can be much taller if the content requires. I’ve included that declaration to make the footer always on the bottom of the page.
