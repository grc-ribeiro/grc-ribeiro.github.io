Learned that you could put grid-template-columns in auto auto. Not sure what the difference between that and 1fr 1fr.

Great question! With 1fr 1fr, the columns will necessarily have the width. With auto auto, the columns may adapt to different contents. Also, for the next assignment, you will need something like grid-template-columns: 15em auto;, meaning that there will be 2 columns: the first will be exactly 15em, while the second will take whatever space is left on the grid.
