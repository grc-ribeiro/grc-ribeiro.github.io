GRC 347: Assignments

Packaging design

This project consists in designing packaging for a fictitious line of product in one of the following categories:

  1. car products
  2. cleaning supplies
  3. oral care
  4. eye care
  5. innerwear
  6. hardware

Your category will be assigned randomly in class.

Competitive analysis

As part of the process, you need to work on a preliminary analysis of the competitive landscape. You want to search for the main brands in your field as well as the not‑so‑known brands, with different, nicer visual language.

Use Pinterest or similar service to collect the images.

Important: Although, Pinterest is great for collecting images, it is awful tool for searching for images. Do not use Pinterest to look for your images. Find better sources and upload them to Pinterest yourself.

Include somewhere from 30 to 80 images on your board. (There’s no penalty for doing more, though.)

The competitive analysis count for 5% of your grade on this assignment.
