GRC 377: Assignments

Magazine spreads

Create a four-page document in InDesign.

For this assignment, you want to use facing pages.

Size: Letter (8.5″ × 11″)

Now, imagine you’re responsible for the design of the Verge’s print magazine. You need to layout this article about Google Duplex.

You probably won’t be able to fit the whole article in four pages, but that’s okay. Just design its first four pages.

Make sure to find images to go with the article, giving them proper credit.

Also, you want to give proper credit to the author of the article.

Highlight some parts of the text with callouts.

Use your best judgement for colors and type size, keeping in mind the questions such as hierarchy and legibility.

Don’t forget to use proper paragraph styles.
