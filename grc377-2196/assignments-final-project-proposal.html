GRC 377: Assignments

Final project proposal

Create a fictional business and design its website. It must be a small business, with only one location. Some examples: restaurant, café, clothing shop, jewelry, pet shop, beauty salon, barber shop, music shop, record store, etc. Suggestion: you can use the fictitious company that you designed a logo for in the first week of class.

Requirements

Introduction

  • Entity (company, organization, etc.)
  • Site description

Target audience

  • Who are the people who use this site?
  • What are their primary task and goals?

Comparative analysis

  • Three examples of three good websites in the same (or similar) industry.
  • Include two screen shots of each website.

Website content

Write the actual content for the website. Don’t use placeholders.

Make sure to break the content in web pages.

Describe the images you will use:
