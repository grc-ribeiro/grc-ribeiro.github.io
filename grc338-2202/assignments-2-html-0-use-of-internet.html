Use of the internet

Think about how you use the Internet.

Do you think you are a typical user, advanced, etc? Why? There’s no wrong answer, but write a paragraph explaining your reasons.

Think about three sites that you visit on a regular basis. Write a paragraph for each of those sites, explaining what makes you keep coming back to them.

Turn in a hard copy in class.

Please apply the principles of good typography to this assignment, keeping it simple.

Don’t forget to include your name on the top of the page.
