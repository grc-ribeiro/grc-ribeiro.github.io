GRC 338: Assignments

Design principles examples

Before each class, you must read about the principles of design from the Universal principles of design book. We will be discussing specific principles every class.

You will be assigned to a different group every class. Your group must find at least two images to will represent the principle assigned to your group.

One image should be of a physical product and the other should show a website.

Write one paragraph for each image, explaining why it is an example of good design and good use of the principle. Use the theory of the book as well as your own words. Submit your paragraph descriptions as online text on PolyLearn. Do not write your name, the name of the principle, date, or anything. Write two and only two paragraphs per submission: in the first paragraph, you write about the physical example; in the second paragraph, you write about the website example.

Each image should be 1200px wide, at least.

Read these tips on how to take a good screenshot on a Mac.

Submit two image to Canvas using this name convention:

  1. The word group, followed by your group number.
  2. A hyphen(‑) (again, not underscore).
  3. Number 1 for the physical example; or 2 for the web example.
  4. An optional sequencial lowercase letter, in case you have additional images for the same example.

(If you want to submit more than one image, just add lowercase letters after the numbers: group1-1a.png, group1-1b.png, etc., but keep the physical examples as #1 and web design examples as #2.)

No example designed by Apple, Google, or Netflix allowed.

Here’s two examples of submissions, so you can have a better idea on how to write your paragraphs and how to name your image files.

You will present your images in class. It’s a very informal presentation, from your own seat, but you are expected to explain the principle assigned to you, using the images as examples
