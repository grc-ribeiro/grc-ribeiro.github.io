Module 10

Type specimen design (cont.)

Learning objectives

In this module we will work on how to:

  1. Apply best strategies to showcase a type family.
  2. Apply proper visual hierarchy to match content hierarchy.
  3. Enhance the typographical grid to increase the visual appeal of typographical layouts.
