GRC 322: Resources

Module 2

Type design (cont.)

Learning objectives

In this module we will continue working on type design.

Before the lab

  1. Watch the video critique of the first submissions of the pixel fonts.

  2. Optional: You may want to start fixing your 9px pixel font.

  3. Optional: You may want to start expanding your 6px and 12px pixel fonts.

Entry ticket

  1. Answer the one-question survey on the video critique.

Come prepared and energized to work on your pixel font!

In the lab

  1. We’ll be working on your pixel font. Expect collaboration with your peers and mini-critiques from me.

  2. In the last 30 minutes of class, we’ll review everybody’s work, so you can make the final changes at home.

  3. You will be assigned your typeface for the next assignment. (Please, remind me if I forget.)

After the lab

Submit the assignment. (Deadline assigned on Canvas.)

And don’t forget to submit your journals!
