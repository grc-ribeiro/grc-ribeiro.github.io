Learning objectives

In this module we will work on how to:

  1. Identify historical type families and their contexts with technology.

  2. Design characters of a typeface.

Before the lab

  1. Read the welcome letter on Canvas.

  2. Watch the video on the abbreviated history of type and complete its quiz on Canvas.

  3. Watch the video on type anatomy and classification and complete its quiz on Canvas.

Entry ticket

Before you come to class you need to complete the following:

  1. Complete the initial survey

  2. Finish the quiz on the topic of abbreviated history of type.

  3. Finish the quiz on the topic of type anatomy and classification.

All due right before your lab time.

Come prepared and energized to work on your pixel font!

In the lab

  1. In the first 20 to 30 minutes of the lab, we’ll have some quick introductions and establish some ground rules for the quarter.

  2. The remaining of the class will be dedicated to working on your pixel font. Expect collaboration with your peers and mini-critiques from me.

  3. In the last 30 minutes of class, we’ll review everybody’s work, so you can make the final changes at home.

After the lab

Submit the assignment. (Deadline assigned on Canvas.)

And don’t forget to submit your journals!
