How to take good screenshots of webpages on a Mac

  1. Hide the Favorites Bar.

  2. Hide the Tab Bar.

  3. Press cmd+shift+4 on your Mac.

  4. Press the space bar.

  5. Take the picture of your browser window using your mouse.
