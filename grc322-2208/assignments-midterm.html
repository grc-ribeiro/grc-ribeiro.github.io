GRC 322: Assignments

Midterm

For the midterm, you will be asked to identify the typeface in twenty different examples.

The examples will be randomly assigned from a question bank, but they will be featuring one of the typefaces below.

Adobe Garamond
Baskerville
Bodoni
Caslon
Clarendon
Didot
DIN
Franklin Gothic
Futura
Interstate
Meta
Minion
Mr Eaves Sans
Officina
Sabon
Sutro
Times New Roman
Trade Gothic

(There’ll be repetition, and it’s unlikely that all typefaces will be featured on the examples shown to you.)

You have two chances to get the best score. But you should get 100% on the first try because you can open the links above and compare the images with the typefaces. (Also, did you know you can customize what’s the word displayed on Adobe Fonts for each typeface, that’s how I got the screenshots for this test.)

If you want to try a second chance, only the best score will count. But the questions will be different since they’re randomly chosen. (Or at least this is my understanding of how Canvas works. If I’m wrong, please let me know.)

You can also work in collaboration with someone through Zoom and take the test together, but again, your questions will be different. But you can take the test for one person and the for another. I wouldn’t call it cheating if it helps everybody to learn more about the typefaces.
