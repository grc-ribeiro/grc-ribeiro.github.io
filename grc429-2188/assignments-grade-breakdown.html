GRC 429: Assignments

Grade breakdown

Project documentation: 60%

Every week—from week 2 to finals week— you will be required to bring an updated version of your report. On finals week, you will also deliver a final presentation on the day of our finals. Those are the only assignments for this class. Your report will be graded weekly, and in each week the grade will receive a greater weight. In the second week, it will weigh 2; in the third week, it will weight 3, and so on, until finals week, when your final presentation will weight 12.

Exams: 30%

Midterm exam: 40%
Final exam: 60%

Class journal: 10%

At the end of every week of class, you need to write a brief journal entry on PolyLearn. It should not take you more than five minutes, though.
