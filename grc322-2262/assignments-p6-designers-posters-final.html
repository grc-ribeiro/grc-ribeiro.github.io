GRC 322: Assignments

Designers posters (final)

With what you learn from your work and my feedback, you want to work on a final version of your 12″× 18″ poster.

Tasks

  1. Watch the video critique and read my comments from the first and second rounds.

  2. Work on your final layout for the poster.

  3. Export the poster as PDF files with and without the grid.

Evaluation

  • Research

    10 points

    Is the research about the designer complete? Did you include the most important facts? Did you proofread your text?

    • 10 pts Satisfactory
    • 8 pts Mostly satisfactory
    • 5 pts Partially satisfactory
    • 0 pts Unsatisfactory

  • Typography

    30 points

    Are the typefaces appropriate? Does the visual hierarchy reflect the content hierarchy? Do the paragraph lines have an appropriate length? Is the type size adequate for legibility? Is the leading adequate for the type size?

    • 30 pts Satisfactory
    • 25 pts Mostly satisfactory
    • 15 pts Partially satisfactory
    • 0 pts Unsatisfactory

  • Images

    20 points

    Good selection of images that are representative of the designer’s work. Do the images have good resolution and good quality? Are they properly cropped? Should they be cropped at all?

    • 20 pts Satisfactory
    • 15 pts Mostly satisfactory
    • 10 pts Partially satisfactory
    • 0 pts Unsatisfactory

  • Grid

    30 points

    Are all the elements properly aligned to the grid? Is the grid being well used to convey planning, intelligibility, clarity, orderliness, and credibility? Elements must be very well aligned within the grid making the layouts organized but also visually interesting.

    • 30 pts Satisfactory
    • 25 pts Mostly satisfactory
    • 15 pts Partially satisfactory
    • 0 pts Unsatisfactory

  • Color and consistency

    10 points

    Is color being used effectively to direct users to attract attention, group elements, indicate meaning, and enhance aesthetics? Are similar parts related? Are related parts similar? Are the variations consistently applied in all parts of each poster? (Posters don’t need consistency in the set, only within each layout. Don’t even think of them as a set; they are supposed to be different. See next criterion.)

    • 10 pts Satisfactory
    • 8 pts Mostly satisfactory
    • 5 pts Partially satisfactory
    • 0 pts Unsatisfactory

Submission

  1. Name the PDF file of your poster without the grid as lastname-final-grid-hidden.pdf.

  2. Name the PDF file of your poster with the grid as lastname-final-grid-show.pdf.

  3. Name the PDF file of your written document explaining the decision‑making process as lastname-design-decisions.pdf.

  4. Submit both files to Canvas.

Please check Canvas for deadline.
