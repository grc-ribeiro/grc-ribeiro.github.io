I recently watched this episode https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=LCfBYE97rFk on one of the designers listed for the next project, Paula Scher, and it brought to my attention important impacts that type can have on society. Type can have economic impacts. It is mentioned how brands need to live and die by their typeface choice. A notable example is Gap changing its iconic serif typeface to a Helvetica typeface, which received immediate backlash from the public, prompting them to change it back within a week. The documentary also mentions the political impact that typography can have and brings up the example of the 2000 “butterfly ballet” in Florida, which had a confusing layout and bad typographical hierarchy that may have led people to vote for the wrong person, likely altering the outcome of the close election and changing the course of American history. This documentary really instilled the idea of how typography can ultimately determine how we navigate the world.
