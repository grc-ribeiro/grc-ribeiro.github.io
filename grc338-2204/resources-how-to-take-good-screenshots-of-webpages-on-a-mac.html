How to take good screenshots of webpages on a Mac

  1. Open the webpage on its own window, with no other tabs.

  2. Hide the Favorites Bar.

  3. Hide the Tab Bar.

  4. Press cmd+shift+4 on your Mac. (Your mouse cursor will become a cross.)

  5. Press the space bar. (Your mouse cursor will become a camera icon.)

  6. Take the picture of your browser window clicking on it with your mouse.
