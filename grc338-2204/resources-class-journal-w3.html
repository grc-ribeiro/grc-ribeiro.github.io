I learned that there is some overlap between the principles we’re learning and sometimes it may be hard to differentiate between two. But, you have to look at specific aspects of a design to figure out which principle(s) the design is using.

Which is more a general rule than a specific quantified measurement. Just keep in mind that any additional highlighting diminishes the effectiveness of highlighting on the other elements.
