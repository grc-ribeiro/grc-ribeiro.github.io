The <address> tag can be used to format not just addresses, but any text to become italicized.

Absolutely not!!! Use the <address> tag for its correct semantics only. I don’t even understand or agree with it being rendered as italic on most browsers.

There is an <address> tag.

According to the excellent MDN, “the contact information provided by an <address> element's contents can take whatever form is appropriate for the context, and may include any type of contact information that is needed, such as a physical address, URL, email address, phone number, social media handle, geographic coordinates, and so forth.”

Why can there be only one h1 tag in each file?

Because that’s the main heading. It’s unlikely that you’ll have two main headings of equal importance. In our case, we use the logo, because its alt attribute will be the name of the business, therefore the main heading.
