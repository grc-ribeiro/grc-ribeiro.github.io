GRC 338: Assignments

Final website: proposal

For this assignment, you will have to create a fictitious, small business to design a website for. It must be a small business, with only one location. It can be a restaurant, cafe, bakery, etc., as long it fits the requirement. (Keep it simple.)

The first assignment for this final project is to write a proposal for your site.

Tasks

  1. Write an introduction of your company, with its name and business it’s in.

  2. Explain its target audience. Who are the people who use this site? What are their primary task and goals? Why would they visit this site?

  3. Research three examples of good websites in the same (or similar) industry.

  4. Take two screen shots of each website.

  5. Write the actual content for the website. Don’t use placeholders. You may borrow the content from other websites. For example, in the About page, you may have the story very much inspired by a restaurant that you know. Or maybe the menu is exactly from another place.

  6. Make sure to break the content in web pages.

  7. You need four pages: Home, About, Menu, and Location.

  8. You need at least eight items in your menu, with title, price, and description.

  9. Don’t include the images you will use, just describe them in square brackets:
    [yellow boat in the ocean]
    (They will eventually be your alt attribute.
