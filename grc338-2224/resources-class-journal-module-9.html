I am still confused why one uses pixels for a border, and why is this the only instance pixels are used as a metric.

Since borders don’t affect the layout when you scale the page, we can use pixels to make them sharper. So you avoid half pixels in such thin lines. Half pixels can’t be rendered by the screen; what it does is to simulate half pixels with tinted pixels, making the line blurry. In retina screens is certainly less noticeable, but still, you know you’re being sharp and precise.
