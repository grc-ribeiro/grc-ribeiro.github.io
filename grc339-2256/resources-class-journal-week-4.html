Make sure to also include an alt text for your logo (on all HTML pages too)! This allows the screen reader to read it as the company website’s name instead of just “image.” There is a legal requirement for alt text under the ADA, Americans with Disabilities Act, meaning that each image should include a descriptive, concise statement for each image. Including a period at the end will allow the screen reader to take a pause.
