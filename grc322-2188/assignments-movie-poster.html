ART 322: Assignments

Movie poster

This assignment will be consisted of making a 24×36″ poster for a movie. The hypothetical movie needs to be based on a fiction book or short novel of your choice, as long as it hasn’t been featured on screens. Also, it must not be a children’s book either.

Constraints

  1. Highlight title, main actors, and director of the movie.

  2. Include writers, producers, other actors, music, etc… You can download this template to help you, but you don’t have to. (Thanks for sharing, Ivy!)

  3. You may use a combination type, lettering, illustration, photograph, or anything you want to represent your movie.

  4. You may use copyright-free images you find on the internet, for example, but you need to make sure it’s at least 150 ppi in the final output.

  5. The MPAA rate logo is required.

  6. A studio logo is required. I’m sharing some here, but you can look for any other studio on Brands of the World.

 

(You are encouraged to have fun with the assignment, but it doesn’t mean you want it to be funny. Avoid jokes, puns, etc.)

Submit your PDF to PolyLearn, by Tuesday, 13 November 2018 at 8:10am, using the name convention Lastname-poster.pdf.
