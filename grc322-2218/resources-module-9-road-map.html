GRC 322: Resources

Module 9

Type specimen design

Learning objectives

In this module we will work on how to:

  1. Select the appropriate type families for a layout.

Before the lab

  1. Watch the video on advanced typographic layout and complete its quiz on Canvas.

  2. Research the type family you’ll be working on your final project.

Entry ticket

Before you come to class you need to complete the following:

  1. Link to the type family page (on Adobe Fonts or the foundry’s website).

  2. Link to your board on Pinterest (or similar service).

  3. Finish the quiz on the topic of advanced typographic layout.

Check Canvas for the different deadline.

Come prepared and energized to work on your specimen!

In the lab

  1. In the first 20 to 30 minutes of the lab, we’ll be looking at type specimen examples, mostly form Emigre.

  2. The remaining of the class will be dedicated to working on your specimen. Expect collaboration with your peers and mini-critiques from me.

  3. In the last 30 minutes of class, we’ll review everybody’s work, so you can make changes at home.

After the lab

Submit the assignment. (Deadline assigned on Canvas.)

And don’t forget to submit your journals!
