GRC 322: Resources

Module 3

Automated typography

Type hierarchy

Learning objectives

In this module we will work on how to:

  1. Write search queries with regular expression to match more advanced patterns.

  2. Explain differences in type families’ design.

  3. Apply proper visual hierarchy to match content hierarchy.

Before the lab

  1. Work on lessons 3 and 4 of RegexOne.com.
  2. Watch the video on type hierarchy and complete its quiz on Canvas.
  3. Watch the video on the type comparison example and complete its quiz on Canvas.
  4. Preliminary research on the history of each typeface assigned to your group.
  5. Collect visual references for both typefaces.

Entry ticket

Before you come to class you need to complete the following:

  1. Finish the quiz on the topic of type hierarchy history of type.
  2. Finish the quiz on the type comparison example and classification.
  3. Submit the preliminary research on both typefaces.

In the lab

  1. In the first 20 to 30 minutes of the lab, we’ll be working on a quick assignment using regular expression, similar to lessons 3 and 4. Submissions will be individual, but collaboration is always encouraged.

  2. In the last 30 minutes of class, we’ll review everybody’s work, so you can make the final changes at home.

  3. You will be assigned what designer you’ll research for the next assignment.

After the lab

Submit the assignment. (Deadline assigned on Canvas.)

And don’t forget to submit your journals!
