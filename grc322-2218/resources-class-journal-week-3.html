This week we learned more about GREP and practiced more techniques for finding specific words and changing the style of them with character styles. This will be very helpful for my book design class and finding certain words or phrases and changing the style of them.

By the way, you can do it directly on the paragraph style, so you don’t need to search for a word. For example, some typefaces need a different font for the lowercase numerals, so you can make on the paragraph style a GREP style to apply a specific character style to all digits.
