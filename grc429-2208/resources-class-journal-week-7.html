Class journal, week 7

While I like the idea of limitless possibilities in the class, sometimes I feel my concepts are not grounded in reality. But rather a movie reality. The technology we have just isn’t quite good enough for some of the features I would like to have implemented into this application. While I know it will eventually advance to the point that this tech should be available, it still feels a bit far fetched currently.

And that’s what makes it more interesting than most projects a potential employer sees in interviews.

I can’t believe this was our last video/quiz. I hope we are not completely on our own for figuring out the rest of the report.

There isn’t much left. But I have office hours every day, you know.

The usability tests were very helpful for our product. We wondered if the swipe mechanism was going to work well, and the test proved that it didn’t. Instead, we found that the users liked to click the buttons instead, so we made a button that said “Next” instead of a swipe.

The problem with swiping is guessability. Hard to know it’s there.

How do we fix our table of contents when the pages do not link to one another?

Make sure you’re using the proper paragraph styles. Then select the ToC and update it.

I actually had a conversation with someone the other day about thispersondoesnotexist.com. They were a little weirded out by the website and I told him I was told about it in class and how it could be used for personas and he was really interested in that because he would’ve never thought of that being a use.

I was weird out by it the first time I saw it too. But students were using it for personas, and I thought it made perfect sense.

Prototyping on Adobe XD is a new skill that I have recently learned.

It’s rather fun, isn’t it? Quite simple and effective.

Adobe XD is my new favorite program. Prototyping is still something I’m learning but they make it really easy to do.

All these prototyping tools are quite fun to use.

Breaking down the steps of the process week by week has made everything much more manageable and gives a great perspective on progress.

That’s the design process.

Crazy how fast this quarter is going! I cant believe our project for this quarter is wrapping up. Honestly its been such a relief that we’ve taken each piece of the project week by week and it would’ve been so overwhelming if it was all assigned at the beginning.

But if you think about it, it was all assigned in the beginning, since the page with all the steps and deadlines was already up. We just have weekly milestones to achieve. In the future, make sure to break projects in milestones if they’re not broken for you.

I wonder if we will end up with a booklet for the final product. Do we have to redesign our report to make it prettier?

No. The report is the final product.

This week our grade in the class dropped quite a bit which made me and my teammates quite nervous.

Every week, the weigh of the grade is a little higher, making it easier to recover in the end.

When should we update our abstract from the first few weeks?

Whenever you feel it needs to be updated.

I think I am struggling to find a typeface I absolutely love. Is it better to make a logo for our app or just use a different typeface for the name of the app?

Don’t spend too long on the logo.

I used Gill Sans for some of the layouts…then read your comment about that typeface in 322 and researching the designer…so I looked him up and I think I’m going to change the typeface for next week’s turn-in because ummmmmmmmm… :o

I would.

My group didnt get much feedback from the usability analysis test so maybe we didnt ask them to do the best tasks.

You don’t want feedback from the test. It’s a test. You want to test your product for usability.

Creating the UI design was a fun process, I realized that if you don’t get inspiration from other existing apps and websites, the user will find the interface confusing because it’s a completely new design and they will be confused.

Yes. Mental models.

Icon is used for recognition, but the name is necessary to avoid potential confusion or misinterpretation.

Exactly.

Placebo effect is real, in my opinion.

Not even an opinion. It has been proved by science.

I looked ahead to week 8. Based on my personal definition of “ethical considerations,” I do not think our project will have too many to consider.

Make sure to read the articles and participate in the discussions, then.

I’m looking forward to choosing a final design for the product! Also with all these report updates, I think this will be a great portfolio piece because there’s so much content showing ideation and the process!

I’ve said it before. This is the project that you’ll use to get a job.
