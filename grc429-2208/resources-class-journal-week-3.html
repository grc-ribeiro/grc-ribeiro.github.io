This made me think of a case study from GrC 320 where a fast food company tried to improve milkshake sales by asking people who fit the demographic they expected of people that enjoyed milkshakes to describe the perfect milkshake, but even when applying that information sales didn’t improve. They realized they were not looking at the real purpose people have when buying a milkshake. After further investigation they realized most of the people buying milkshakes bought them first thing in the morning, and after more questioning they learned that many people were buying milkshakes as an easy on the go snack for their morning commute to work.
