GRC 322: Resources

Module 8

Working with tables

Learning objectives

In this module we will work on how to:

  1. Apply typographic principles to table design.

  2. Apply proper visual hierarchy to match content hierarchy.

Before the lab

  1. Watch the video on table design and complete its quiz on Canvas.

  2. Optional: read the final project description and begin your research on a type family.

Entry ticket

Before you come to class you need to complete the following:

  1. Finish the quiz on the topic of table design.

In the lab

  1. The following 60–90 minutes will be dedicated to working on your table. Expect collaboration with your peers and mini-critiques from me.

  2. In the last hour of class, you’ll be introduced to the final project. You want to leave the lab with your type family chosen.

All due the night before lab at 11:59pm.

Come prepared and energized to work on your table!

After the lab

Submit the assignment. (Deadline assigned on Canvas.)

And don’t forget to submit your journals!
