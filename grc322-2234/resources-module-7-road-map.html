GRC 322: Resources

Module 7

Automated typography

Research poster design (cont.)

Learning objectives

In this module we will work on how to:

  1. Write search queries with regular expression to match more advanced patterns.

  2. Enhance the typographical grid to increase the visual appeal of typographical layouts.

Before the lab

  1. Work on lessons 11 and 12 of RegexOne.com.

  2. Watch the video critique of the first submissions of the grid poster.

  3. Optional: Start working on your new layouts for the poster, based on the critique for the first round.

Entry ticket

Before you come to class you need to complete the following:

  1. Answer the one-question survey on the video critique.

  2. Submit screenshots of your best attempt to complete RegexOne.com lessons 11 and 12.

All due the night before lab at 11:59pm.

Come prepared and energized to work on your poster!

In the lab

  1. In the first 20 to 30 minutes of the lab, we’ll be working on a quick assignment using regular expression, similar to lessons 11 and 12. Submissions will be individual, but collaboration is always encouraged.

  2. The remaining of the class will be dedicated to working on your poster. Expect collaboration with your peers and mini-critiques from me.

  3. In the last 30 minutes of class, we’ll review everybody’s work, so you can make the final changes at home.

After the lab

Submit the assignment. (Deadline assigned on Canvas.)

And don’t forget to submit your journals!
