GRC 322: Resources

Module 2

Introduction to automated typography

Type design (cont.)

Learning objectives

In this module we will work on how to:

  1. Write search queries with regular expression to match basic patterns.

  2. Compare two similar typefaces.

Before the lab

  1. Work on lessons 1, 1½, and 2 of RegexOne.com.

  2. Watch the video critique of the first submissions of the pixel fonts.

  3. Optional: You may want to start fixing your 9px pixel font.

  4. Optional: You may want to start expanding your 6px and 12px pixel fonts.

Entry ticket

  1. Answer the one-question survey on the video critique.

  2. Submit screenshots of your best attempt to complete RegexOne.com lessons 1, 1½, and 2.

Due the night before lab at 11:59pm.

Come prepared and energized to work on your pixel font!

In the lab

  1. In the first 20 to 30 minutes of the lab, we’ll be working on a quick assignment using regular expression, similar to lessons 1, 1½, and 2. Submissions will be individual, but collaboration is always encouraged.

  2. The remaining of the class will be dedicated to working on your pixel font. Expect collaboration with your peers and mini-critiques from me.

  3. In the last 30 minutes of class, we’ll review everybody’s work, so you can make the final changes at home.

  4. You will be assigned your typeface for the next assignment. (Please, remind me if I forget.)

After the lab

Submit the assignment. (Deadline assigned on Canvas.)

And don’t forget to submit your journals!
