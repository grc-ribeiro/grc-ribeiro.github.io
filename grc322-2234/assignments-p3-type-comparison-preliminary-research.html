Tasks

  1. Do a preliminary research on your typefaces.

  2. Write a preliminary text (between 100 and 300 words) for each typeface. Include facts such as who design it, when, and where. Also, explain if there was a specific purpose for its design and the historical context of its creation.

Submission

One submission per student at this point.

Submit it to Canvas as a text file, using the name convention lastname-type-comparison-research.txt. (Replace lastname with your actual last name, please.)
