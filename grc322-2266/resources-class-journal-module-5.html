GRC 322: Resources

Class journal, week 5

Mood boards are really helpful when starting a design. It makes it much easier to find a starting point.

Don’t design without them. And use them consciously.

Collecting reference images for the type specimen pamphlet was very helpful in bringing the pamphlet to life.

Always, always, always have your references before any design work.

Overall, throughout the quarter, I enjoyed checking out the resources and websites linked; it created a toolkit for me when designing.

Make sure to build more references and check them often!

In the future, I want to familiarize myself with more credible design journals to gain inspiration.

More than journals, look for the work of great designers. Especially the young ones.

I just took the final. I thought it was really interesting because I really had to pay attention to the details in the serifs, bowls, stems, etc.

And if you do, you can get them all correct.

I can’t believe it’s already week five, this class went by so fast!

I hope you have enjoyed it!
