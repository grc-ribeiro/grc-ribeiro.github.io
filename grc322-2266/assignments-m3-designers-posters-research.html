Tasks

  1. Research the life and work of the designer assigned to you. Submit the preliminary research (300–500 words) on Canvas before your lab.

  2. Collect 12 to 30 images in high resolution. Submit a zip file on Canvas before your lab.

  3. Make sure you research the type families you’re planning on using. You want to use well regarded type families. The excellent Fonts In Use website can be a very helpful starting point.
