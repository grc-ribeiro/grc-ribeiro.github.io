I learned how valuable it can be to take assignments in steps, rather than all at once. Online classes (and normal classes) really highlight the importance of time management and how much easier assignments get when worked on in smaller portions.

Time management is of course an essential skill to have. And working in steps makes it easier indeed. There’s a reason for that. There’s only so many hours on a day that you can devote to intelectual work. Nice tip of productivity: When you have some time but you are tired and can’t do meaningful intelectual work anymore, look for mechanical things you can do. For example, in my case, sometimes I don’t have the energy to grade, but I can download files, put them in the right folders, check if they need to rename, etc. When I’m ready to grade, the files are ready too.
