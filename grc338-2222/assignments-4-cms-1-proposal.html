GRC 338: Assignments

CMS website: proposal

For this website, you will create a fictional business and design its website using Squarespace. (Click here to watch the video on why you’ll be using a website builder.) It’s from two years ago, but it still works.

It must be a small business, with only one location. Some examples: restaurant, café, clothing shop, jewelry shop, pet shop, beauty salon, barber shop, music shop, record store, etc. (A design, photo, or art studio is not allowed.)

Important: You want to make it very different from your previous assignment, so you have different websites on your portfolio.

The first assignment for this final project is to write a proposal for your site.

Tasks

  1. Write an introduction of your company, with its name and business it’s in.

  2. Explain its target audience. Who are the people who use this site? What are their primary task and goals? Why would they visit this site?

  3. Research three examples of good websites in the same (or similar) industry.

  4. Take two screen shots of each website. (If you don’t have access to a Mac to take that screen shot with drop shadow and all, do your best with the presentation of such screen shots.)

  5. Write the actual content for the website. Don’t use placeholders.

  6. Make sure to break the content in web pages.

  7. Describe the images you will use:
    [yellow boat in the ocean]

Submission

Rather than submitting a PDF or MS Word document with your proposal, you will submit a webpage.

  1. Please download this template and populate with the content of your proposal.

  2. Replace my screen shots of the GrC’s website with your references.

  3. Make sure to read all the instructions marked as comments on the HTML document.

  4. Publish your proposal on the URL: username.github.io/grc338/website-proposal

  5. Submit a fun emoji to Canvas letting me know you have submitted it. Then, I can grade your proposal and open the next step.

  6. Click on your name to check if it’s working.
