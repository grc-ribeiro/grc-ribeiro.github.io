Could you please remind me, is there a big advantage for using <div> vs. <section>?

The biggest advantage is semantic. In HTML5, several tags such as <article>, <aside>, <header>, <footer>, and <section>, were introduced. They work just as <div>, but for more specific uses.
 
From the (excellent) MDN web docs:
 
“Each<section> should be identified, typically by including a heading (<h1>-<h6> element) as a child of the <section> element. Do not use the <section> element as a generic container; this is what <div> is for, especially when the sectioning is only for styling purposes. A rule of thumb is that a section should logically appear in the outline of a document.”
 
Makes sense?
