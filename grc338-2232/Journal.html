At the end of every module of class, you need to write a brief journal on Canvas. It should not take you more than five minutes, though. The main point of the journal is to write down what are the main points you have learned, being from lectures, discussions, or lab activities. You may have learned content from the instructor, another student, or looking it up on your own. They’re all valid.

I will start every week by sharing with the class the highlights from the journal entries.

Even though I will be able to know who wrote each entry, this information won’t be shared with class, and the entries will be shared anonymously. If you submit a valid tip, I want to give you credit. (e.g.: “Thanks for sharing, Alex!”) If you want your tip to be shared anonymously, please let me know when you share it.

You may also include in the journal:

Links to some valuable resource you found.

Your next steps on the project you’re working on.

Topics you want to investigate next.

Concepts that have remained confusing.

Please take the time to write three to eight bullet points.
