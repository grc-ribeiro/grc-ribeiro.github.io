My digital magazine is about chairs so I thought I’d share a fun fact. The average person spends up to 17,000 sitting in a chair.

17,000 what? Hours of their lives?

Being a little paranoid in general and assigning checkpoints to each major thing finished and making a copy of the code.

Nothing wrong with being extra safe. But make sure you don’t end editing different files.

Designing when you are indecisive is not easy.

To design is to make a decision. If you’re not sure, you can ask for someone else’s opinion. You don’t need to follow their opinion, but once you hear it, you’ll likely see if you agree or not.

I would love to experiment with the appearance of elements on hover next.

That’s a good idea. It can be quite cool as an add‑on. But keep in mind that there’s no hover in touch devices.

I’m learning that some colors look good in theory but not in practice on websites.

Makes sense.

I’m realizing that the color palette that I am using for my 440 magazine isn’t translating as well in website form, so I’m pivoting a bit.

Makes sense.

Thanks for sharing!
