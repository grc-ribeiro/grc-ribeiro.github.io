GRC 322: Assignments

Designer poster survey

For the next assignment, you will design a poster for a designer. To have a better idea, you can download my example from Canvas so you know what you’re going to design.

Usually, I assign the designers randomly, but this quarter, I want to experiment with students choosing their preferred designers after doing some quick research.

Here is a list of designers for you to choose from. Look them up and choose the top 5 you would like to be featured in your poster. Keep in mind, though, that you won’t design a poster emulating your designer’s style. You will design a poster featuring your designer’s work. (Think a magazine page featuring a story about them.)

At this point, however, you really don’t need to worry too much about the details of the next assignment. Just look up as many or as few as you want from the list below and make note of the ones you found more interesting. I should say, though, that every good designer that I know is a design nerd. If you want to be good at design, you want to be familiar with as many designers as you can.
