Grade breakdown

Project documentation: 60% ###

Every week—from week 2 to finals week— you will be required to bring an updated version of your report. On finals week, you will also deliver a final presentation on the day of our finals. Those are the only assignments for this class. Your report will be graded weekly, and in each week the grade will receive a greater weight.

Design process

Design process and design principles

Class journal: 10% ###

At the end of every week of class, you need to write a brief journal entry on PolyLearn. It should not take you more than five minutes, though. I will start every week by sharing with the class the highlights from the journal entries. Even though I will be able to know who wrote each entry, this information won’t be shared with class, and the entries will be shared anonymously. (Unless you submit a joke; jokes will be shared with credit to the student who submitted them.)
