GRC 429: Assignments

Homework (week 1)

In this course, you will work for 10 weeks on the same project. In groups of two students, you will create a new product that uses augmented reality to help someone do their job. This person will have one of the following jobs:

  1. Bus driver
  2. Firefighter
  3. K–12 teacher
  4. Librarian
  5. Nurse

For this first week, each group needs to choose a first and a second choice from the list above. Then write two paragraphs for each choice answering the following questions:

  1. Besides convenience, why did you choose that person’s job to help?

  2. Speaking of convenience, how do you plan to have access to people that have that job? (You’ll need to interview five or six of them.)

Submit your ideas on Canvas (as plain text) by Friday, 10 January 2020, at 11:55pm.
