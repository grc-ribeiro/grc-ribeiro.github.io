Type comparison

Poster

After working on your research and presentation, choose what graphics were more effective when you taught us the differences and similarities between the two typefaces you worked on.

Design a 24″×36″ poster that informs about those differences and similarities. Include some facts and a brief history of the typeface, but emphasize similarities and differences.

Make sure to pay attention to all the details on typography that we have covered in this class and you have learned in GRC 318, including:

Typeface selection, leading, line length, kerning, tracking, grid, baseline grid, type anatomy, type classification, type hierarchy, indentation, hyphenation, ragged lines, widows, orphans, proper punctuation characters, old-style figures

Check your spelling eight times at least. Then have one or twelve other people checking it for you.

Submit your poster as PDF to PolyLearn, by Thursday, 18 October 2018 at 8:10am, using the name convention lastname1-lastname2-poster.pdf. Use alphabetical order by last name. And mind the spelling of all names.
