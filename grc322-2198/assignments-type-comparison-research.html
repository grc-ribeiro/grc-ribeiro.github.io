GRC 322: Assignments

Type comparison

Research

For this assignment, each group will be assigned with one pair of typefaces from this list:

Bodoni vs Didot

Franklin Gothic vs Trade Gothic

Adobe Garamond vs Minion

Helvetica Neue vs Aktiv Grotesk

Sabon vs Times New Roman

Clarendon vs Sutro

DIN vs Interstate

Rockwell vs Serifa

Meta vs Officina

Baskerville vs Caslon

Futura vs Mr Eaves Sans

The first part of the assignment is to extensively research and compare the two typefaces.

Your report must be portrait and letter‑sized. For the main text use a typeface that is very different from both typefaces you are researching. Suggestion: if your typefaces are sans‑serif, use a serif typeface for your copy; and vice‑versa.

You must include:

  1. History of each typeface

    For each typeface, include facts such as who design it, when, and where. Also, explain if there was a specific purpose for its design and the historical context of its creation.

  2. Comparison

    Explain, through images and diagrams, what are the biggest similarities and big differences between the two typefaces assigned to your group.

  3. Examples and visual references

    Research images where the use of the typefaces were particularly successful. Fonts In Use can be a very helpful starting point.

You can download two examples from last year on PolyLearn. You will be able to read my comments to the students and avoid the mistakes they made on their otherwise very good work.
